How can I get the discount from the coupon in the store?

You may click on the banner. You will be taken to the other webpage and after filling up the form you may print out the coupon image. If you show the coupon image to the seller in the store you will get a discount of your choice.

I would like to reclaim the product. Is it possible?

Yes! You may contact our customer service in the address customerservice@schoolsupplies.com or a seller in one of our stores. They will gladly help you.

What methods of payment do you accept?

In all our stores you may use all major Czech credit card brands or pay in cash.

What are the opening hours of the stores?

During working week all stores are opened from 9 am to 6 pm. All stores are closed during weekends and holidays.

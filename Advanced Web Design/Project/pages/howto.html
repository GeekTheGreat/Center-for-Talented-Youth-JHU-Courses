HOW TO...

... choose the right school bag

  1. Get a bag that is large enough to hold all your things.
  2. Find out how durable you want your back to be.
  3. Get a bag from reputable brand.
  4. Choose bag that is appealing.

... pack a pencil case for school

  1. Get the pencil case of size you need.
  2. Buy all necessary supplies.
    Children may need:
    1. Three pencils and pens
    2. Scissors
    3. Glue Stick
    4. Highlighter
    5. Color Pencils
